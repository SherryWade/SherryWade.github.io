HOUSE CAST PART-TIME POSITIONS:

House Cast members play fully scripted, highly comic roles in continually running productions. As a cast member, you would be expected to memorize at least one full-length play, to prepare with limited rehearsal, and to be available to perform weekly at least two weekday evenings & at least two weekends every month. (You may work at other jobs/theatres as well as long as you are consistently available to us.) Cast members average 5-10 performances per month, and are paid well per performance with opportunity for raises.

IMPORTANT NOTE ON YOUR AVAILABILITY:

Please only audition if your schedule is generally open so that you are available to us twice a week AS WELL AS two weekends a month. (Again, you may work other jobs/theatres as long as you are consistently available to us as well.)
