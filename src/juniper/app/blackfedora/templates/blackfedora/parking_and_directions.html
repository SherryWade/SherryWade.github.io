Parking and Directions

Location:

The Black Fedora Comedy Mystery Theatre and Shoppe makes its home in historic downtown Charleston, South Carolina.

We're located at 164 Church Street, between South Market Street and Cumberland Street.

We're a block from the downtown City Market and are adjacent to the French Quarter Inn & Tommy Condon's Restaurant.

Parking:

We're conveniently located near a number of parking lots. A city parking garage is across the street from the theatre at the corner of Cumberland and Church streets.
